About Me

I am a Computer Science researcher with a PhD from the University of Texas at Austin, specializing in image generation and computer vision. My research focuses on developing novel approaches to generative models and their applications in creative AI systems.

With expertise in deep learning, computer graphics, and machine learning, I work on cutting-edge problems in image synthesis, style transfer, and generative adversarial networks. My research has applications in digital art, content creation, and visual computing.
